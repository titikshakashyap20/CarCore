POPULAR CATEGORIES

Your guide to understanding what makes cars tick

ENGINE

An engine is the heart of a car, converting fuel into power to drive the vehicle. It combines air, fuel, and spark in a series of controlled explosions to generate energy for motion.

BRAKES

Brakes are essential safety components that slow down or stop a vehicle by converting kinetic energy into heat through friction, ensuring controlled and safe driving.

BATTERY

Car batteries provide the electrical power needed to start the engine and supply energy to the vehicle's electrical systems when the engine is off.
